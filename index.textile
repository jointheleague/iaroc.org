h2. 5th Annual International Autonomous Robot Competition (iARoC), June 23 - June 24, 2012,  San Diego

h3. Prizes! Excitement!

$1000 First Prize $500 Second Prize $250 Third Prize 

h3. LOCATION: San Diego County Fairgrounds 

h3. DATE AND TIME: June 23 - June 24, 2012

* RoboCamp  Monday - Friday, June 18 - 22, 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM
* iARoC            Saturday - Sunday,  June 23 and 24, 2012 - 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM 

See "schedule":schedule.html for details.
