h2. 6th Annual International Autonomous Robot Competition (iARoC)

h3. Prizes! Excitement!

$1000 First Prize $500 Second Prize $250 Third Prize

h3. LOCATION: Reuben H. Fleet Science Center at Balboa Park, San Diego

h3. DATE AND TIME: June 29 - June 30, 2013

* RoboCamp  Monday&ndash;Friday, June 24&ndash;28, 1:00 PM&ndash;8:00 PM (5:00 PM on Friday)
* iARoC            Saturday&ndash;Sunday,  June 29&ndash;30, 9:00 AM&ndash;5:00 PM

See "schedule":schedule.html for details.

h3. Current Standing
Congratulations to all the teams who participated in the 2013 iARoC!
We had a great competition with lots of fun!
The highlight was seeing one robot, Team NortherNerds, outside the competition demonstrate mapping of the maze!
